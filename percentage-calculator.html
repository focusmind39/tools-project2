Introduction to the Percentage Calculator Tool

Percentages are a fundamental part of daily math. Whether you are a business owner calculating sales tax, a marketer analyzing campaign growth, a student working on algebra, or a consumer checking product discounts, percentages are a constant requirement. The free **Percentage Calculator** by Enginewheels provides three separate calculation modules to handle different percentage queries instantly.

Many online calculators process calculations on their backend servers, which means your figures and inputs are transmitted over the network and could be saved in database logs. Our tool operates entirely client-side. All calculations run locally in your browser using JavaScript, keeping your numbers private.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the formula to calculate percentage increase or decrease?
The formula is: ((New Value - Original Value) / Original Value) * 100. A positive result represents a percentage increase, while a negative result represents a percentage decrease.
How does the tool handle decimal values?
Our calculator handles floating-point math and outputs results rounded up to four decimal places. Unnecessary trailing zeros are stripped to keep values clean.
Can I input negative numbers into the calculator?
Yes. Our tool supports both positive and negative values, allowing you to calculate percentages for negative growths, balances, or drops.
Is my calculation data secure and private?
Absolutely. All math is processed locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your numbers are never uploaded to our servers, keeping your data private.
Why can't I calculate the percentage increase from zero?
Mathematically, calculating a percentage increase from zero is undefined because it requires dividing by the initial value (which would be zero). The calculator will alert you to prevent this calculation.
